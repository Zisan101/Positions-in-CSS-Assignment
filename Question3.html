Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, is regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time. He began his professional career at FC Barcelona, where he spent over two decades and won numerous titles, including a record eight Ballon d'Or awards. Known for his exceptional dribbling, vision, and goal-scoring abilities, Messi has achieved global fame. In 2021, he moved to Paris Saint-Germain before joining Inter Miami in 2023. He also led Argentina to victory in the 2022 FIFA World Cup, cementing his legacy in football history
